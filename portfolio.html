ePortfolio

This is a portforlio for my capstone project at SNHU and shows some of the projects I have completed.

Completing the capstone project and my coursework for my bachelor's degree has helped me both learn and show what I am capable of as a software engineer. Specifically, the full-stack web app below shows an ability to work with databases (nonrelational in this case) as well as an ability to work in a cloud environment with Docker for containerization. Both the web app project as well as my games (check out the games tab in the menu for more) show an ability to design and develop software. A big part of developing software is deciding what algorithms and data structures to use. To show I am capable of this, I have shown the Cave Game. In this project, I use DFS to generate randomized caves and the Marching Squares (2D marching cubes) to render the cave walls.

While there was some collaboration and communication with stakeholders in my academic experience, these skills are better shown through my professional experience. Particularly during my time at Marching Cube (now GameSim, Austin) and Amazon, I worked on small teams of engineers, regularly collaborated both within my team and other teams, and communicated with stakeholders regularly.
